## Disclaimer

> This mod gamifies environmental perception methods that some individuals
> with severe visual impairments may use. It is not a representation of all
> blind or visually impaired people's real experiences, nor a complete
> simulation of medical or life conditions.

## Compatibility Notes

- Map/minimap mods (Xaero's, JourneyMap, FTB Chunks, etc.) are blocked before entering worlds.
- Block-info HUD mods (Jade, WTHIT, WAILA, etc.) are blocked.
- JEI, REI, EMI are allowed.
- This is an experience-integrity check, not security-grade anti-cheat.
